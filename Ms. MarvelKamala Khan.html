Ms. Marvel/Kamala Khan

Kamala Khan did for the American Muslim
community what Black Panther did for the
African American. In 2013, Kamala Khan,
aka Ms. Marvel made her first appearance
and was vastly popular. At the time there were
still extreme racial prejudices against Muslims
and people from the Middle East, with the phrase
“go back home” frequently shouted at individuals
who were actually born and raised in the U.S.
Although Kamala does not wear a Muslim Hijab,
she does shatter Western stereotypes of Muslim
women. In fact, she perpetuates the fact that though
she grew up in a Muslim household and her family
is from Pakistan, America is her home.
