Disability

Disability in the media is often misrepresented and
overexaggerated. The blind and deaf are often portrayed as
having their other senses immensely enhanced because one is
missing. However, this is not the case. Marvel’s Daredevil series
and the character of Maya Lopez, aka Echo, portray disability
more accurately. Daredevil’s senses are only enhanced because of
radioactive waste, and Echo’s, through her training. These
superheroes helped derail “media depictions which deployed
sentimentality and a ‘pity model’ … for the unfortunate”
(Alaniz 71). I would be remiss however not to mention the
Hollywood scandal regarding the character of Hawkeye. Deaf in
the comics, his cinematic counterpart most definitely was not.
This step backwards in representation of disabeled characters to
promote the idea that only able bodied white people could be
superheros was met with immense backlash. It was a stark
reminder of the silencing that minority and disabled communities
are forced to face.
