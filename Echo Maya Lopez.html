Echo/Maya Lopez

Maya Lopez, aka, Echo is the intersection of disability
and minority in the Marvel Universe as she embodies
three underrepresented communities: that of the deaf,
of Native Americans and women as heroes. Lopez,
descended from the Cheyenne Nation, witnesses the
death of her parents at an extremely young age and
is taken in by the man who murdered them. Bilingual,
she uses both American Sign Language and the Native
American Sign Language System to communicate. Her
character is vastly important for those of both
communities as they are often used as cliche
plot devices: to be killed off to advance the story
line. Because of Echoâ€™s success. there has been a
rise of deaf superheroes and deaf representation
