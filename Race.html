Race and Gender

The Marvel Comic and Cinematic Universe spans across space,
time and reality. One would think that the concept of race would
not be a point of contention in its fictional universe as it is in
reality. Sadly, heroes of different race and gender have been met
with backlash solely for being a different gender or non-white,
especially when released in theatres. In spite of the hate however, Black
Panther’s (2018) massive success paved the way for Marvel
Studios to release more of its diverse heroes: Shang-Chi (2022),
and Marvel’s Disney+ show Ms. Marvel (2022). Marvel
pioneered with the Black Panther, once more in a time of intense
racial unrest, and once again, Marvel shows just how important it is to shatter
stereotypes and represent the silenced.
