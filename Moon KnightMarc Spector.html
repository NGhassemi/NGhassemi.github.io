Moon Knight/Marc Spector

Marc Spector, aka Moon Knight, is often described as Batman with Dissociative Identity
Disorder. His comics not only involve the hero battling supervillains, but also his own
psyche. Unlike most superheroes, even others with mental disorders, Marc Spector does
not have one single origin story. There are various stories of Spector either being granted
his powers from an Egyptian God or, believing he had super powers to the point of
actually developing them through his fictive personality based on Captain America,
Wolverine, Spiderman, etc. Some of these stories include childhood trauma, others do
not. This provides a sense of realism to the character, depicting DID as a defense
mechanism rather than portraying it as an illness.
