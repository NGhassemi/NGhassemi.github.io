Mental Illness

Superheros all come with a backstory to explain how they got their powers. This backstory can also explain how mental illnesses developed in the heroes we know and love. Mental illness has been prevalent in the Marvel Comics Universe nearly since its inception. Captain America, among the earliest of Marvel’s heroes, sought psychological counseling for PTSD in the comics from the 1960’s and ‘70’s. There are many other characters in the comic universe that prominently display mental illness. There are heroes with schizophrenia, depression and anxiety. Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and other Marvel authors have never ignored these struggles. Yet they do not make the mistake common in popular culture which tends to over exaggerate mental illness. Books, TV shows and movies with characters who suffer from a form of Mental illness generally tend to fall into harmful tropes and stereotypes.
